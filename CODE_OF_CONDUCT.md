# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in SentinelX a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening a GitHub Issue or contacting the maintainer directly.
